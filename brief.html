Brief (for authors only)

This is the master .Rmd document that will eventually be used to tie all the sections together for the final book chapter. “to do” lists, ideas and conventions can be written here, but it's main purpose is to provide an up-to-date structure on the final chapter.

The sections are preliminary; it is recommended that the sections are written as separate .Rmd files before merging them all into one master document to reduce complexity and time taken to compile.
